Conduct an interview with schoolmates and friends about their views of what HCDC is. Analyze and classify their answers according to the philosophy of HCDC.

Respond #1 from a Bachelor Arts Communication Specializing Social Communication:

Located in Davao City, Holy Cross of Davao College (HCDC) is a renowned educational institution known for its commitment to quality education and holistic student development. With a rich array of activities and clubs, HCDC cultivates competitiveness through competitions and fosters confidence and character growth. The institution's emphasis on well-rounded education prepares graduates not only academically but also socially, ensuring they excel in various aspects of life. HCDC stands as a beacon of excellence, shaping students into capable and principled individuals ready to face the world's challenges.

As a freshman at HCDC, I've always admired the school's commitment to providing a high-quality Catholic education that every student dreams of. HCDC excels not only in its dedicated faculty, scholars, and students, but also in fostering a strong spiritual foundation. By guiding students along a path enriched with faith, HCDC ensures that each individual's journey is accompanied by the presence of God.

Not only that but also build strong connection with everyone through activities and clubs to enhance confidence and character growth, he also said that the institution helps student grow to prepare them later in life which is aligned to the mission and vision of HCDC.

Quest 8: Summary

The Holy Cross of Davao College (HCDC) is a Catholic institution dedicated to forming a community of believers and evangelizers committed to truth, education, and the creation of a more compassionate world. They place a premium on offering high-quality Catholic education as well as cultivating excellence and holiness. They also aim to foster a human and Christian learning environment conducive to social reform.

The spirituality of HCDC is built on engaging with God as a way of life. Stewardship is stressed, with individuals being called to live virtuous lives, mature as followers of Jesus, and bring others closer to God. Conservation, living a basic lifestyle, campaigning for ecological, practicing spiritual discipline, and accepting co-responsibility are all examples of values.

HCDC's basic values are Truth, Justice, Peace, Service, and Wisdom. Truth is regarded as the highest achievement since it entails the discovery of God. Justice is regarded as the highest consciousness, calling people to be just and agents of justice. The greatest source of inspiration is peace, which is attributed to God. The biggest obligation is to encourage people to become servant-leaders and followers of Jesus Christ. Finally, Wisdom is the most significant commitment, as it requires building a right relationship with God.
